Presage Audit — Deep Dive

What a full audit actually looks like.

Module 01
Menu & Product Performance
We scan all available reviews, social mentions, and community posts for item-level signals. What's praised, what's ignored, what's being requested but not offered. If your best item isn't on the front of your menu or ads — that changes. If your worst item is dragging your brand — we tell you whether to fix, relaunch, or cut it.
Review ExtractionItem ScoringLaunch Strategy
Module 02
Inventory & Supply Chain
We review your vendor structure for consolidation opportunities, pricing misalignment, and local sourcing gaps. We identify whether you're overpaying, undersourcing regionally, or missing KC-area farms and producers that could reduce cost and increase quality simultaneously.
Vendor AnalysisCost ReviewLocal Sourcing
Module 03
Competitor Gap Analysis
We map what competitors are winning on and identify what they're leaving open. If a business two blocks away has a fan-favorite that your menu doesn't touch — we surface it, score the demand, and build a launch strategy for your version. Their weakness is your opportunity.
Competitive MappingGap IdentificationOpportunity Scoring
